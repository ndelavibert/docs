## Before you begin

You will need access to the email inbox that should own the Synckony workspace.

## Create your account

1. Open the Synckony sign-up page.
2. Enter your work email address and create a password.
3. Verify your email address from the confirmation email.
4. Sign in and create your workspace.

## Recommended next steps

- Invite your team members before connecting integrations.
- Keep your workspace name aligned with your business or store name.
- Continue with the [quickstart](/quickstart) after your account is active.
